Features that makes us Unique

As in our curriculum, we emphasize more in improving the following sectors:

Science

BCNSHS provides a well-rounded science education that equips students
with a strong foundation in various scientific disciplines.

Technology

BCNSHS prepares students for the digital age and the evolving demands
of the workforce.

Engineering

BCNSHS aims to develop students' analytical thinking, creativity, and
problem-solving abilities within the field of engineering.

Mathematics

BCNSHS provides a rigorous and comprehensive mathematics program
that aims to develop students' mathematical reasoning, analytical
thinking, and problem-solving abilities.
