About Our School

Baguio City National Science High School follows the K-12 education system in the Philippines, offering a specialized curriculum that emphasizes STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) subjects. Our school provides a conducive learning environment with well-equipped laboratories and facilities to support hands-on learning experiences.

Our school also offers a range of academic programs and extracurricular activities to foster the holistic development of its students. These include research competitions, science fairs, math contests, and participation in regional and national science Olympiads. The school also encourages community involvement and values formation through outreach programs and various clubs and organizations.

Over the years, Baguio City National Science High School has produced numerous successful graduates who have excelled in various fields, including science, engineering, medicine, and research. The school's commitment to academic excellence has made it one of the top science high schools in the region, attracting students who have a passion for scientific exploration and innovation.
