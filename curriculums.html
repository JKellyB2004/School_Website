Curriculums Offered in BCNSHS

  • STEM Grades 7-10 (Junior High)

    Students in this program engage in rigorous coursework that covers a wide range of scientific disciplines and fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills.

  • STEM (Senior High)

    This course prepares students for higher education and careers in STEM fields through advanced coursework, practical laboratory experiences, and opportunities for research and innovation.

  • Humanities and Social Sciences

    Engage in subjects such as literature, social studies, philosophy, psychology, economics, and other disciplines that foster critical thinking, communication skills, and a deeper understanding of human society and culture.

  • Accounting, Business, and Management

    Provides students with a strong foundation in business, finance, accounting, and management principles.

  • AUTOMOTIVE

    Various aspects related to automobiles, such as automotive technology, mechanics, maintenance, repair, and other relevant subjects.

  • Information and Communication Technologies

    Gain practical skills and theoretical understanding to pursue careers in fields such as software development, cybersecurity, systems analysis, and technology management.

  • Animation

    InvolveS the study of various aspects related to animation, including principles of animation, digital art, storytelling, character design, 2D and 3D animation techniques.
